Join The ILS Network

This isn't open enrollment.

Integrated Life Styles — The ILS Network — isn't open enrollment. It's a curated group of entrepreneurs, executives, media professionals, and builders who back each other, in the room and outside it.

If you're actively building or leading something and want the right people around you while you do it, tell us about it.

What That Looks Like

  • Connect — introductions to partners, collaborators, and mentors already inside the network;
  • Create — a platform and support for your ideas, content, and projects; and
  • Lead — masterminds, workshops, and real-world collaboration with people building at your level.

How It Works

This isn't a form that adds you to a list. Tell us what you're building or leading and what you're looking for. We review every request personally — no open enrollment, no automated approval. If it's a fit, we'll follow up directly with next steps.
